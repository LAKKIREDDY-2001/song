Vibestream Music Upload Folder
==============================

Place your physical .mp3 or .wav audio files in this directory! 
Vite will automatically expose them under the static paths.

How to reference them:
--------------------
1. Drag and drop your mp3 files right into this "/public/music" folder using your Google AI Studio file explorer.
2. Open "/src/data/customSongs.ts".
3. Add an entry matching your filename. For example, if you uploaded "my_song.mp3", configure your object like this:

{
  name: "My Custom Song",
  artist: "Your Artist",
  img: "img_custom",
  audio: "my_song", // references public/music/my_song.mp3
  imageUrl: "https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1514525253161-7a46d19cd819?w=600",
  category: "Custom",
  durationStr: "3:45"
}
